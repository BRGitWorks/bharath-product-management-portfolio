Day 1 – Motorola Downfall

Product

Motorola consumer mobile handsets (early 2000s RAZR era).

Target Users

Style-conscious consumers, early adopters of mobile hardware.

UVP

Iconic slim design and aspirational branding at a time when form-factor mattered most.

Analysis / Strategy

  • Failure to shift quickly to touchscreen + ecosystem (OS, app platform) vs Apple/Android leaders.
  • Hardware-first focus without services made differentiation transient.
  • Strategy: invest in software/OS & developer ecosystem or partner tightly with a platform early.

Day 2 – Blinkit vs Swiggy SWOT

Product

Blinkit — instant grocery delivery app.

Competitor

Swiggy Instamart (and other quick commerce players).

UVP

Sub-30 or 10–15 minute deliveries in dense urban catchment.

Analysis / Strategy

  • Strengths: speed, recognition via parent brand, focused micro-warehouses.
  • Weaknesses: thin unit economics; high fulfillment costs.
  • Strategy: hybrid fulfillment windows, subscription loyalty to improve unit economics.
